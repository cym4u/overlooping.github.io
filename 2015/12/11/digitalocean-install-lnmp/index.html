Chown operation not permitted for root
http://serverfault.com/questions/60711/chown-operation-not-permitted-for-root

root@vps:/home/wwwroot# chown -R www /home/wwwroot/www.tencent.vc
chown: changing ownership of ‘/home/wwwroot/www.tencent.vc/.user.ini’: Operation not permitted

解决方案：
root@vps:/home/wwwroot# chattr -ais /home/wwwroot/www.tencent.vc/.user.ini
root@vps:/home/wwwroot# chown -R www /home/wwwroot/www.tencent.vc
